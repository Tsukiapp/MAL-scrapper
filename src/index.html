Avex Pictures opened an official website for the television anime adaptation of Tomoko Yamashita's Sankaku Mado no Sotogawa wa Yoru (The Night Beyond the Tricornered Window) manga on Thursday, and revealed the main cast, staff, first teaser visual (pictured), and teaser promotional video. The anime is scheduled to premiere in October 2021.

Cast
Kousuke Mikado: Nobunaga Shimazaki (Mashiro no Oto)
Rihito Hiyakawa: Wataru Hatano (Shadows House)

Staff:
Director, Character Design: Yoshitaka Yasuda (Tsugumomo OVA chief animation director, Nil Admirari no Tenbin main animator)
Chief Director: Daiji Iwanaga (Doukyonin wa Hiza, Tokidoki, Atama no Ue. episode director)
Series Composition: Ayumi Sekine (IDOLiSH7)
Studio: Zero-G

Yamashita serialized the supernatural mystery manga in Magazine Be x Boy from March 2013 until last December. Libre published the tenth and final compiled volume on March 10.

VIZ Media licensed Sankaku Mado no Sotogawa wa Yoru in English under their SuBLime imprint in September 2014, and will ship the eighth volume on July 27.
